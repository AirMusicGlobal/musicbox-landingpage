know who your
listeners are

Music is all about connection. With MusicBox, you will never lose connection, but you will connect with people on a level you might have never imagined.


Stream your content and share that awesomeness with people around the world. If you feel like celebrating, make someone on another continent happy too, and become the life of the party! On the other hand, if you feel a little bit lonely or your mood has just gone down, you can always open our innovative map feature. That way, you will see you are never alone because someone somewhere on this beautiful planet is jamming to your stream with you.

A new way of connection is waiting for you! Join the music revolution and set someone's mood for the day, discover, sing, dance, share and listen along with other people around the globe.

A GOOD PLAYLIST A DAY
KEEPS THE BAD MOOD AWAY

Know that nagging feeling of having to flip through song after song until you find one melody that will fit your mood perfectly? Yeah, we don’t like it either. That is why AirMusic lets you create the perfect unique playlists.


Add songs with zero effort and create your favorite libraries that you can play anytime. AirMusic playlists are completely customizable so make yours fit your character perfectly. Rock the day with some AC/DC or lift your mood with Harry Styles! Enjoy effortless access to songs, playlists, podcasts and radio channels.

Discover new music or trending albums and create your own awesome library.
